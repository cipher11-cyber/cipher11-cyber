MAC(media access control) ADRESS:


Why change MAC adress:
1) Increase anonymity.
2) Impersonate other sevices or copy other device MAC adress.
3)Bypass filters.

MAC spoofing = is the chnaging of MAC adress not physically.

ifconfig (interface) down = temporary turns off the interface.
ifconfig (interface) hw ether (MAC ADRESS) = change mac adress.
ifconfig (interface) up = turns on the interface



systemctl restart NetworkManager = restarts the Network manager.
